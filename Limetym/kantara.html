The film stars Rishab Shetty in a dual role as a Kambala champion who is at loggerheads with an upright forest officer named Murali (played by Kishore). Set and filmed in Keradi in coastal Karnataka, principal photography began in August 2021.[23] The cinematography was handled by Arvind S. Kashyap, with B.
